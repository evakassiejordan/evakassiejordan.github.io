Our Story

Why Momo?

Momo means peach — and pink — in Japanese. It represents the soft boldness we bring to every campaign: feminine energy, fearless strategy, and a colour palette that refuses to blend in.

Founded on the Cornish coast at 21, Momo Collective bridges Japanese fashion sensibility with British seaside grit. We don't just manage social media — we curate identities, build communities, and turn casual scrollers into loyal customers.

Whether you're a heritage fashion house or a local café, we believe every brand deserves a feed that feels like art.
